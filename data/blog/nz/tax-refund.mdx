相信很多朋友結束紐西蘭打工度假後，都會忘記退稅這件事，或以為要等到4月退稅月才能拿回稅收，但也可以提早申請退稅唷！

1. 先登入 MyIRD

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/1.png)

2. 登入後點擊 I want to

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/2.jpg)

3. 點擊 send a message

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/3.jpg)

4. 點擊 income tax

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/4.jpg)

5. 點擊 credit and refund

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/5.jpg)

6. 輸入訊息資訊，這應該是最齊全的資料提供了，每一項聲明都需寫上,並提供機票及最後一張payslip的證明。

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/6.jpg)

```
Title ： Request for tax refund in advance

Hello, I am a working holiday visa holder and would like to apply for a tax refund in advance.The following are my details.
- Name: xxx
- IRD Number: 000-000-000
- Oversea Address: xxx
- Oversea Contact: +60xxxxxxx
- Tax refund bank account:  xx-xxxx-xxxxxxx-xx

- I departed from NZ on January 18, 2024, and the airline ticket is attached.
- I have received my final pay, and the payslip is attached.
- I‘m citizen of Malaysia, and I will be leaving New Zealand permanently.
- My tax residency status will be non-resident after I leave NZ.
- I will not have any source of income after I leave NZ.

If there's any missing information, feel free to contact me.Thank you!
```

7. 提交完成就等消息啦。

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/7.jpg)

8. 不用等15天，我隔天就收到訊息了，退稅也會在兩天內匯款完成。

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/8.jpg)

9. 再隔天會收到兩封信
   ![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/9.jpg)

10. 第一封為退稅估算表，可以了解總收入及最後退稅金額。
    ![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/10.jpg)

11. 第二封為註銷IRD account 通知。
    ![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/11.jpg)

12. 收到退稅金啦，如實在兩天內收到唷，非常有效率。

![](/static/images/nz/tax-refund/12.jpg)

不用等到紐西蘭4月退稅，馬上就可以先退稅拿到錢啦，希望幫助到有需要的人～
